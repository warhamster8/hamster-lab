Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 9, 2026

FocusDeck is a fully local application. We do not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information, usage metrics, diagnostic telemetry, or analytics data.

1. Personal Data

All folder choices, files processed, and settings remain strictly on your local computer.

2. File Processing

Image loading, renaming, conversions and resizing run entirely on your machine. No files are uploaded to external servers.

3. Third-Party Services

FocusDeck does not integrate with advertising networks, tracking libraries, or analytics platforms.

4. Contact

Questions? Reach us via the Support page or GitHub Issues.
