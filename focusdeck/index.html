Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 25, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how FocusDeck processes and handles your data. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it.

1. Personal Data & Information Collection

FocusDeck is a fully local application. We do not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information, usage metrics, diagnostic telemetry, or analytics data. All your actions, including folder choices, files processed, and optimization settings, remain strictly on your local computer.

2. File Processing

All image loading, renaming, conversions, and resizing calculations are performed entirely on your local machine. FocusDeck does not upload your files to any external servers or third-party web services.

3. Third-Party Services

FocusDeck does not integrate with any third-party advertising networks, tracking libraries, or analytics platforms.

4. Updates & Changes

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated modification date.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us at the support page provided on this website.

Frequently Asked Questions:

Q: What file formats are supported?
A: FocusDeck supports RAW formats (RAF, CR2, NEF, DNG, ARW, ORF, RW2, etc.) and standard formats (JPEG, PNG).

Q: Can I use it offline?
A: Yes! FocusDeck runs entirely locally on your machine and does not require an active internet connection to process photos.
